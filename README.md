# 👋 About Me

🔭 **I'm currently working on**
Leading and delivering Unity games across mobile, PC, console, and VR. My focus is gameplay systems, multiplayer architecture, performance optimization, and platform-compliant ports for Nintendo Switch, PS4/PS5, Xbox, Android, iOS, WebGL, and Meta Quest.

🏆 **Highlight**
First engineer in Pakistan to officially ship and certify a Nintendo Switch title through Nintendo Lotcheck (Life in Willowdale: Farm Adventures). I've also shipped and optimized games that improved performance by 25 to 50% and rescued unstable inherited codebases.

🌱 **I'm currently learning**
Advanced multiplayer optimization, deeper Unity Job System and ECS concepts, and faster iteration workflows for live multiplayer games across platforms.

👯 **I'm looking to collaborate on**
Indie or AA games with backend-driven gameplay (Photon, PlayFab), technically challenging projects that need clean architecture and scalable systems, or stabilizing and rescuing inherited codebases.

💬 **Ask me about**
Unity gameplay architecture, Azure PlayFab, console porting and certification, performance optimization, multiplayer debugging, and leading Unity teams.
